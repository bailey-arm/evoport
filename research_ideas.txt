Research Ideas for evoport
==========================

Evolutionary Algorithm Enhancements
------------------------------------
- NSGA-II / NSGA-III: crowding distance and reference-point selection for better-spread frontiers
- Crossover operators: BLX-alpha or SBX crossover between two parents instead of single-parent perturbation
- Adaptive mutation: decay noise_scale over generations or make it self-adaptive
- CMA-ES / MO-CMA-ES: learn covariance structure of weight-space for smarter search

Portfolio Theory Extensions
----------------------------
- Transaction costs & turnover penalties as an objective
- Cardinality constraints: limit number of non-zero weight assets (sparse portfolios)
- Short selling: LongShort population class with net exposure / gross leverage bounds
- Risk parity / equal risk contribution objective
- Black-Litterman views: incorporate subjective return forecasts as a prior

Risk & Objective Functions
---------------------------
- Conditional Drawdown at Risk (CDaR)
- Ulcer Index (drawdown-duration-weighted risk)
- Higher moments: skewness and kurtosis objectives
- Regime-aware objectives: condition metrics on HMM-detected market regimes

Backtesting & Evaluation
--------------------------
- Benchmark-relative metrics: tracking error, information ratio, active share
- Rebalance frequency analysis: daily vs weekly vs monthly
- Expanding vs rolling window systematic comparison
- Robustness testing: bootstrap / Monte Carlo resampling of returns

Domination & Selection
-----------------------
- Hypervolume indicator for frontier quality measurement
- Tournament selection with crowding distance for smoother pressure
- Decision-making under uncertainty: stochastic dominance, minimax regret

Data & Assets
--------------
- Alternative asset classes: crypto, commodities, bonds
- Factor-based returns: optimize over Fama-French factors
- Correlated asset generation: add correlation structure to synthetic data
